Frequently Asked Questions

I operate natively within your existing communication and project management tools. The integration process begins with a quiet audit phase where I observe workflows without interrupting them. Once systems are mapped, rollouts are done sequentially with complete documentation.

Standardization moves from chaos to structure in three phases. First, I document the current state. Second, I optimize the process and remove redundant steps. Finally, I write the formal SOP and embed it directly into the task management system.

AI is deployed to handle the initial heavy lifting of repetitive tasks. By building strict prompt templates and automated intake forms, we allow generative models to draft copy and analyze data, shifting the team to editing and approving.

A comprehensive audit generally takes one to two weeks depending on organizational complexity. Restructuring begins immediately after, with core project management systems and primary SOPs established within the first thirty days.
